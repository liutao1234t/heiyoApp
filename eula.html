Community Guidelines & EULA

Last updated: June 8, 2026 · Contact: support@heiyo.xyz

These guidelines apply to all uploads, AI try-on results, community posts, comments, expert chats, messages, reports, and interactions on Heiyo.

Heiyo is built for positive hairstyle exploration, respectful beauty discussion, and safe AI try-on experiences.

1. Community Purpose

Heiyo is intended to help users explore hairstyles, share beauty inspiration, ask style questions, view AI try-on results, and participate in a positive beauty-focused community.

Users must follow these Community Guidelines in addition to Heiyo’s Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

2. Respectful Conduct

Users must treat others respectfully. Harassment, bullying, intimidation, threats, hateful conduct, stalking, malicious false reporting, or repeated hostile behavior are prohibited.

Users may not attack or degrade people based on race, ethnicity, nationality, religion, sex, gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, medical condition, age, immigration status, veteran status, or similar protected characteristics.

3. Photo Consent and Privacy

Do not upload another person’s image without permission. This includes friends, strangers, customers, models, public figures, minors, or people appearing in group photos.

You may not share another person’s private information, including addresses, phone numbers, email addresses, private messages, IDs, payment details, or confidential salon/customer information.

4. AI Try-On Rules

Users may not use AI try-on features to create harmful, deceptive, harassing, sexual, exploitative, illegal, impersonating, or privacy-invasive content.

Users may not attempt to bypass safety systems, manipulate AI behavior, generate misleading identity-related content, or use AI outputs to defame or deceive others.

5. Expert Chat Rules

Expert chat and community messaging must remain respectful and beauty-focused. Users may not harass experts, pressure users, solicit illegal services, send spam, request sensitive personal data, or use chat features to abuse others.

Advice received through Heiyo is general beauty guidance and should not replace professional medical, dermatological, or salon consultation where appropriate.

6. Prohibited Content

  • Illegal, harmful, violent, threatening, or exploitative content.
  • Hate speech, harassment, bullying, or targeted abuse.
  • Sexually explicit, pornographic, exploitative, or non-consensual intimate content.
  • Scams, phishing, impersonation, misleading promotions, or fake identities.
  • Content that exposes another person’s private information.
  • Copyright-infringing photos, images, logos, or designs.
  • Spam, bots, fake engagement, scraping, or platform manipulation.

7. Beauty Safety Disclaimer

Heiyo content is for digital visualization and inspiration. AI hairstyle previews do not guarantee real-world results or cosmetic safety.

Users should consider allergies, scalp sensitivity, hair condition, bleaching risks, dye reactions, and professional salon advice before making real-world hairstyle decisions.

8. Intellectual Property

Users should only upload photos, hairstyle references, logos, images, or creative materials that they own or are authorized to use.

Unauthorized use of copyrighted photos, celebrity images, salon works, brand logos, or third-party materials may result in removal or account enforcement.

9. Consumable Purchases and Fair Use

Consumable digital items must be used honestly and in accordance with platform rules. Users may not exploit purchase systems, manipulate item delivery, abuse refunds, or use purchased items to harass or manipulate others.

10. Reporting and Moderation

Users are encouraged to report harmful content, AI misuse, privacy violations, copyright infringement, impersonation, harassment, scams, or unsafe behavior.

Heiyo may review reports using manual review, automated tools, safety signals, purchase records, moderation history, and community reports.

11. Enforcement

Heiyo may take enforcement action at its discretion, including warnings, content removal, reduced visibility, messaging restrictions, AI generation restrictions, purchase restrictions, temporary suspension, permanent account termination, or preservation of records for safety and legal purposes.

Severity, repetition, harm level, legal obligations, and App Store compliance requirements may affect enforcement decisions.

12. Contact

Questions about these guidelines may be sent to support@heiyo.xyz.
