Heiyo Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 8, 2026 · Contact: support@heiyo.xyz

This Privacy Policy explains how Heiyo collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects information when you use our hairstyle discovery, AI try-on, community, expert chat, and related services.

Heiyo uses internally trained AI models for hairstyle visualization and does not collect facial biometric templates or use uploaded photos for identity recognition.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information you provide

  • Account information: username, email address, profile photo, bio, age or date of birth, hairstyle preferences, saved looks, and other information you choose to provide.
  • Uploaded photos: photos voluntarily submitted for hairstyle try-on, profile images, community posts, or support requests.
  • AI try-on information: selected hairstyles, hair colors, uploaded references, generation settings, output history, feedback, and non-sensitive interaction signals used to provide and improve AI features.
  • Community and messaging content: posts, comments, messages, reports, likes, follows, expert chat messages, and feedback.
  • Support and safety information: support communications, report reasons, screenshots, moderation appeals, and information needed to investigate safety issues.
  • Purchase information: consumable product identifiers, transaction identifiers, purchase status, receipt validation data, delivery status, and purchase support records.

1.2 Information collected automatically

  • Device model, operating system, app version, language, time zone, IP address, diagnostics, crash logs, network information, and device identifiers where permitted.
  • Usage data such as pages viewed, hairstyle categories browsed, try-on activity, saved styles, comments, likes, messages, reports, purchases, and search activity.
  • Security and fraud signals such as login records, moderation history, spam signals, abuse reports, and purchase risk signals.

1.3 Device permissions

Heiyo may request access to camera and photos so you can upload images for hairstyle try-on or community sharing. We may request microphone access if voice or video-related features are provided. We may request notification permission for account, message, activity, generation, safety, or purchase notices. You can change permissions in device settings.

2. AI Model and Photo Processing

2.1 Internally trained AI model

Heiyo uses internally managed AI models trained and optimized for hairstyle try-on and beauty visualization scenarios. The model is designed to apply hairstyle shapes, hair colors, wig-like effects, and styling previews onto user-provided photos.

Our model focuses on hairstyle simulation, placement, segmentation, color rendering, and visual blending. It is not built to identify users, verify identity, or determine sensitive personal attributes.

2.2 No face data collection or biometric profiling

Heiyo does not use uploaded photos for face recognition, biometric identification, facial authentication, surveillance, law enforcement profiling, or sensitive attribute inference. We do not create facial biometric templates, faceprints, identity databases, or long-term biometric profiles from uploaded photos.

2.3 Limited processing purpose

Uploaded photos are processed only to provide the user-requested hairstyle try-on, display generated results, support content moderation, troubleshoot service errors, maintain platform security, prevent misuse, and operate the Service.

We do not sell face photos or use them for unrelated advertising targeting. We apply reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect uploaded images and AI-related records.

3. How We Use Information

  • To provide the Service: create accounts, display profiles, enable uploads, provide hairstyle discovery, generate AI try-on results, operate expert chat, and maintain community features.
  • To operate AI features: process photos, selected styles, generation settings, feedback, and safety signals to create hairstyle outputs and improve reliability.
  • To personalize content: recommend hairstyles, colors, community posts, experts, saved collections, and style categories based on interactions and preferences.
  • To process consumable purchases: validate receipts, deliver digital items, maintain records, prevent fraud, and respond to purchase issues.
  • To maintain safety: detect harmful content, harassment, impersonation, privacy violations, AI misuse, fraud, spam, and unauthorized access.
  • To communicate with you: send service messages, security alerts, expert chat notices, support responses, purchase confirmations, and policy updates.
  • To comply with law: respond to lawful requests, enforce Terms, resolve disputes, protect rights, and satisfy platform, accounting, or consumer protection requirements.

4. How We Share Information

We may share information with service providers that help us operate Heiyo, including hosting, cloud storage, analytics, customer support, crash reporting, payment verification, content moderation, security, fraud prevention, and infrastructure providers.

We may share purchase-related information with app stores and payment platforms to verify purchases, deliver consumable items, resolve purchase issues, and prevent fraud.

We may disclose information when required by law, regulation, court order, lawful government request, or when necessary to protect the rights, safety, and security of users, Heiyo, or the public.

We do not sell personal information in the ordinary sense. If applicable law defines certain disclosures as a “sale” or “sharing,” we will provide rights and choices required by that law.

5. Consumable Purchase Data

Heiyo may offer consumable digital items. We may receive purchase-related information such as product identifier, transaction identifier, purchase status, receipt validation data, country, delivery status, and refund or support records.

Consumable purchases are not auto-renewable subscriptions and do not automatically renew. Purchase data is used to deliver items, prevent fraud, maintain records, handle support, and comply with legal or accounting requirements.

6. Data Retention

We retain information only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy unless a longer period is required or permitted by law.

If you delete your account, we will take steps to delete or anonymize personal information, subject to reasonable delays and exceptions. We may retain limited information to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, prevent fraud, preserve moderation records, protect users, or maintain security.

Backup copies may persist for a limited time before being overwritten or deleted.

7. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, object to processing, withdraw consent, or request portability of certain personal information.

  • You may update certain profile information in the app.
  • You may request account deletion by contacting support@heiyo.xyz.
  • You may revoke camera, photo, microphone, notification, or other device permissions through device settings.
  • You may contact us regarding access, correction, deletion, or privacy-related requests.

For security, we may need to verify your identity before processing certain requests.

8. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures designed to protect information against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, alteration, and disclosure. These measures may include access controls, encryption in transit where appropriate, monitoring, internal policies, and security reviews.

No mobile or internet service can guarantee absolute security. You are responsible for protecting your account credentials and device security.

9. International Transfers

Your information may be processed and stored in countries other than where you live. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards for international transfers, such as contractual protections, technical safeguards, and organizational controls.

10. Children’s Privacy

The Service is intended for users who are at least 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we learn that such information was collected, we will take appropriate steps to delete it or handle it as required by law.

11. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, AI model use, photo processing, account deletion, or privacy rights, contact us at support@heiyo.xyz.
